**HR Analytics Dashboard**

An interactive HR Analytics Dashboard designed to analyze employee attrition and uncover workforce trends.
The dashboard enables HR teams to identify key drivers of attrition and make data-driven decisions to improve employee retention and workforce planning.

*The dashboard provides interactive insights into:*

Overall employee attrition

Attrition by age group

Attrition by education background

Attrition by salary range

Attrition by job role

Attrition by years at company

Attrition by gender

**Key Features**

✔ Interactive Dashboard with multiple HR insights
✔ Attrition Rate Analysis across different workforce segments
✔ Dynamic KPIs such as employee count, average age, and salary
✔ Visualizations for quick insights into employee trends
✔ Data-driven insights for HR decision making

**Dashboard KPIs**
Total Employees

Total Attrition

Attrition Rate

Average Employee Age

Average Salary

Average Years at Company

**Tools & Technologies Used**

Power BI – Dashboard creation and visualization

Data Analysis – Workforce data exploration

Data Visualization – KPI tracking and insight generation

**Business Impact**

This dashboard helps organizations:

Identify high-risk attrition groups

Improve employee retention strategies

Support HR workforce planning

Enable data-driven HR decisions
